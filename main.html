Mumbai


Mumbai is a city located on the Konkan coast on the west coast of India123. It was built on seven separate islands that were united into one in the 17th and 18th centuries4. Mumbai is the most populous city in India and the fourth most populous city in the world24. It is also the wealthiest city in India and has the highest number of millionaires and billionaires among all cities in India1. The culture of Mumbai is a blend of cuisines, languages, food, fine arts, cinema, and festivals5. The city has a deep natural harbor and was named an alpha world city in 200812.
